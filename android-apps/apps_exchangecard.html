跪求明信片

描述:

这是一个通过明信片来交友应用，你可以给陌生人恩赐明信片，也可以从别人那里跪求明信片。

  • 向陌生人索取明信片
  • 給陌生人寄送明信片
  • 统计展示收到或发送出去的明信片

隐私保护措施:

本应用不会与任何第三方分享用户私密信息。


Exchange Card

Description:

This app helps to exchange postcards.

  • Register, leave the mail address and get cards from stranger
  • Send cards to others to raise your chance to get one yourself
  • Send more cards to rank high on the list

Privacy Policy:

This app is a standlone app to receive feeds. No personal users data will be collected.
